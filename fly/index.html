Fly me to the moon;
Or fly me to the stars.
Wherever it may be;
And wherever you may go;
Please take me in your flying machine;
Take me far away.

For even though I will be leaving joy;
I can leave the sorrow.
Though I will lose the ones I love;
So too the ones who hate me.

So please;
I ask of you;
Fly me to the moon;
Or fly me to the stars;
Just take me away.

Away from here.
