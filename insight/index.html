Insight.
The ability to reach in and pull out a semblance of an explanation.
It is only useful to ourselves, unable to be put into words.
Existing only as abstract ties of threads of thought, barely held together.
Ready to snap at the slightest tension.

An insight into the world of the unknown.
The ability to perceive something deeper in the world around you.
Knowing that everyone else lives in ignorance, not knowing what lies beneath, what is to come.

It is simply a waste.
