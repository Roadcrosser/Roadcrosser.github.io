A hermit crab settled into his shell;
And thought some seaweed would make it look pretty.
But the shell's demands only grew and grew;
Just screaming for this and that.

The hermit crab worked hard to adorn this shell;
Not worrying about the strain on his health.
"What a beautiful shell!", exclaimed the passing fish,
unable to see the hermit crab.

Even though they were one and the same;
a consciousness subservient to his own shell;
Only living for its sake.
